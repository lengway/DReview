Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DReview.

What is DReview?

DReview is a platform where users can read and write reviews for movies and games.

What is review?

Review - an evaluation of a publication, product, or service. A review can be positive, negative, or neutral. Reviews can be found in newspapers, magazines, websites, and other media. They are often written by experts in the field or by consumers who have used the product or service. But on DReview, reviews are written by ordinary users like you.

How can i write my own review?

In the first you should sign up and then sign in. After that you can go to the movies or games page, choose the movie or game you want and click on the "Write a review" button.
